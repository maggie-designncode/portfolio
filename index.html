About

Deliberate by nature

I'm a UX designer with over five years of experience in B2B finance. I sit at the intersection of high-stakes systems and human behavior — turning dense, expert-level workflows into experiences that feel intuitive.

I tend to overthink things — and I've made peace with that. What looks like overthinking is really just unwillingness to accept a vague problem. I ask questions until the real problem surfaces, then I design.

I work best end-to-end: shaping roadmaps, running research, designing, and testing with real users. I've done this solo and alongside PMs, engineers, and traders who speak a very different language.
